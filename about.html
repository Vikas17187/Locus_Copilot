About Locus Copilot

A data-driven location intelligence platform built to help entrepreneurs find the best spots for their business in Chennai.

The Problem

Choosing where to set up a business is one of the most critical decisions an entrepreneur makes. Most rely on gut feeling, word of mouth, or expensive consultants. Locus Copilot changes that by bringing objective, multi-criteria analysis to the table -- layering real rent data, crowd density, competition saturation, and transit accessibility into a single weighted score.

How It Works

The platform divides Chennai into a grid of 744 micro-localities, each scored across four dimensions. Users set custom weights, choose a business type, and get Haversine-accurate ranked recommendations within any search radius. ML-powered features include K-Means zone clustering, AI-optimized weight profiles per business type, and personalized picks based on search history.
