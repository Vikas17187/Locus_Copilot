About Locus Copilot

A data-driven location intelligence platform built to help entrepreneurs find the best spots for their business in Chennai.

The Problem

Choosing where to set up a business is one of the most critical decisions an entrepreneur makes. Most rely on gut feeling, word of mouth, or expensive consultants. Locus Copilot changes that by bringing objective, multi-criteria analysis to the table -- layering real rent data, crowd density, competition saturation, and transit accessibility into a single weighted score.

How It Works

The platform divides Chennai into a grid of 744 micro-localities, each scored across four dimensions. Users set custom weights, choose a business type, and get Haversine-accurate ranked recommendations within any search radius. ML-powered features include K-Means zone clustering, AI-optimized weight profiles per business type, and personalized picks based on search history.

Where It Works Best (Use Cases)

These business scenarios map directly to our scoring engine and business-type optimization settings:

Medical

Neighborhood Clinic Launch

Prioritize areas where healthcare demand exists and supporting amenities are strong, while avoiding weak transit pockets.

Best for: Clinics, diagnostic centers, pharmacies.
Restaurant

Fast Casual Outlet

Find high-footfall corridors with manageable rent pressure and dense nearby demand signals from POI activity.

Best for: Cafes, cloud-kitchen hubs, dine-in concepts.
Laptop

Electronics Service Desk

Target productivity and education clusters where users repeatedly need repair, accessory, and upgrade support.

Best for: Repair stores, accessory retail, tech kiosks.
Mobile

Mobile Service Point

Focus on dense, well-connected routes where customer churn is high and same-day service speed matters.

Best for: Phone repair, SIM support, accessories.
Automobile

Workshop Expansion

Choose zones with road connectivity and practical access while balancing operational cost and demand coverage.

Best for: Mechanic shops, quick-service garages.
Stationery

Education Cluster Store

Identify localities near schools and coaching ecosystems where daily utility purchases drive repeat business.

Best for: Books, exam prep, print and supply shops.
